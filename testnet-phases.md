## Rollout Roadmap

### **Testnet Launch Components**
The public **testnet** has launched with the following:

- **One logical sequencer** consisting of multiple co-located physical servers for crash fault tolerance, maintained by MegaETH.
- **Multiple replica nodes**, maintained by MegaETH.
- **Multiple full nodes**, maintained by MegaETH.
- **EigenDA as the data availability (DA) service.**

### **Mainnet Launch Components**
Upon **mainnet launch**, MegaETH will operate with the following:

- **One logical sequencer**, maintained by MegaETH.
- **Replica nodes (permissionless)** → Professionally maintained clusters run by MegaETH and third-party service providers.
- **Full nodes (permissionless)** → Anyone can run a full node.
- **Prover nodes (permissionless)** → Enables fraud proofs and validity proofs.
- **EigenDA as the DA service** → Ensuring robust data availability.

 **Both the testnet and mainnet will continue to exist after launch**, serving distinct purposes for development and production use.
